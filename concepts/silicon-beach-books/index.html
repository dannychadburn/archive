A fresh face in digital publishing

Silicon Beach Books compliments an already thriving training company.

The mission is to help people develop their digital knowledge by giving respected professionals a platform from which to share their own experience.

Expert insight and practical advice is offered through an extensive library of single-topic books. They're designed to give busy people reliable information they can actually do something with.

Brighton is full of people who have a potential book in them. By harnessing the knowledge and networks of experienced businesspeople, entrepreneurs, startup founders and talented specialists, we can create a market-leading product with worldwide reach.

Details

Creating income from information

  • Inspirational people are also busy people. They don't necessarily have time to put a book together, so we'll extract their thoughts and ghostwrite it for them, adding in additional insight, research and references.
  • Books will be co-branded, providing reach to the networks of those involved and opportunities (through in-book advertising) to meet the objectives of the publisher and contributor.
  • Sold through an online store (modelled roughly on abookapart.com), books would be optimised for all popular eReader formats, with print-on-demand options available for those who'd prefer a physical product.

Why are we approaching Silicon Beach Training?

  • Building on an exisitng platform - eBooks already form part of a strategy to generate interest in your services. A publishing arm will allow you to expand this approach and further complement your courses.
  • Access to local knowledge - as as established business, your brand offers a foot in the door with the people who we want to collaborate with.
