Concept

A digital publishing brand

Silicon Beach Books offers clear and comprehensive reading on a variety of digital business topics via an online store.

Details

Creating income from information

  • Brighton is full of experienced businesspeople, entrepreneurs, startup founders and talented specialists, each of whom have a potential book in them. By harnessing their expertise, we'll create a market-leading product that's relevant to a huge audience.
  • They're busy people who don't necessarily have time to put a book together, so we'll extract their thoughts with interviews and ghostwrite the book based on their insight, adding in additional research and references.
  • The completed books will be co-branded, providing reach to the networks of those involved and opportunities to meet the objectives of the publisher and contributors.
  • Sold through a dedicated online store (modelled roughly on abookapart.com), books would be optimised for all popular eReader formats, with print-on-demand options available for those who'd prefer a physical product.

Why are we approaching Silicon Beach Training?

  • Building on an exisitng platform - ebooks already form part of a strategy to generate interest in your services. Silicon Beach Books will allow you to expand this approach and complement your courses.
  • Access to local knowledge - as as established business, your brand offers a foot in the door with the people who we want to collaborate with.

Benefits

Big reasons for you to get involved

  • Minimal involvement required - the whole process (research, scoping, specification, development, editing, design, upload, release, promotion) will be taken care of by people with extensive experience in digital publishing.
  • An alternative income stream - traditional business book publishers have been slow to adapt and embrace digital platforms, so there's a lucrative opportunity for someone who can generate useful content and market it effectively.
  • Deeper relationships with experts - a different way of communicating with talented people may open up opportunities to attract new trainers.

Rewards

Why it'll be worth your while

  • A share of the profits - depending on the needs and motivations of the various contributors, each book will have a financial model that's built to benefit everyone involved.
  • Access to new customers - every book marketed creates a new prospect for your core training services (and vice versa), proving a taster for potential customers and added value to those you've already trained.
