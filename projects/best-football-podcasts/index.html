    What is an OPML file?

    Put simply, it's a list of content feeds. You can import them into your podcast player of choice which subscribes you to all of them in one go, rather than having to subscribe to each individually, saving you loads of time.

    You should be able to find some easy-to-follow instructions by searching "Import OMPL into X" on Google. Email if you're having difficulties.
